Domine and Nunito Sans

Opposites Attract

"In the battle of moderns, ITC Century and Futura juxtapose ornament and sleek lines."

The combination of Domine and Nunito Sans is meant to replicate, to an extent, the relationship between ITC Century and Futura. The opposing qualities in each font compliment each other directly. The juxtaposition between Nunito Sans' linear and geometric lettering, and Domine's thick, rounded typography creates a cohesion that provides balance.

Nunito Sans is a terminal typeface that removes any rounding and provides a more strict serif font. On the other hand, Domine provides a squared inside to it's rounded letters and a soft curve to it's curved letters to create a more "airy" typeface.
